Crystals

Collecting crystals can be a fun and interesting hobby. Some consider crystals as a form of living Mother Earth. Many crystals form from melted molten lava that cools and creates various combinations of minerals, resulting in crystals. Others grow organically within the Earth’s surface. Crystals can have many benefits to your health, but for most, it is the beauty of the crystal that catches their eye.

On this website you’ll learn about different types of crystals, their characteristics, and a bit of where to locate them as well. Click on the links below to learn more about the different crystals.
